Author : Gege Akutami

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a gripping Japanese anime/manga series set in a world where cursed spirits threaten humanity. Yuji Itadori, an ordinary high school student, becomes entangled in this world after consuming a cursed object to save his friends. He joins Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School to train as a sorcerer and exorcise cursed spirits.

Alongside his classmates, including the stoic Fushiguro and the brash Nobara, Yuji battles terrifying creatures while uncovering dark secrets about his own powers and the nature of curses. With stunning animation and intense action, it's a must-watch for anime enthusiasts.
