Author : Akira Toriyama

"Dragon Ball" is a beloved Japanese anime series that chronicles the adventures of Goku, a Saiyan warrior with incredible strength and a pure heart. Beginning with his childhood exploits and training under Master Roshi, the series evolves through multiple sagas, including Dragon Ball Z and Dragon Ball Super.

Filled with intense battles against formidable foes like Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu, Goku and his friends strive to protect Earth and the universe from destruction. Alongside the action-packed fights, the series explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and self-discovery, capturing the hearts of fans worldwide with its dynamic characters and epic storytelling.
